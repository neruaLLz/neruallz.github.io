Dita_Note_1

short descriptions 的内容应该是具体的，而不是笼统的，不是 self-referential。

Incorrect:

Coffee harvesting and processing The following information describes how coffee cherries are harvested and processed to become coffee beans that you can use to make espresso drinks.

Correct:

Coffee harvesting and processing To create the coffee used in espresso drinks or brewed coffee, the fruit of the coffee plant is harvested, hulled, cleaned, aged, and finally ground.
